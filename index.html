About This Dashboard

Question: Can an electric semi-truck run the Texas Triangle freight corridor (I-10/I-35/I-45) today?

Methodology:

  • A 25-mile buffer was drawn around I-10, I-35, and I-45.
  • Sample points were placed every 10 miles along each highway.
  • For each point, the nearest DC Fast charger within the corridor was found.
  • Points 30 miles or more from a charger are flagged as Charging Deserts — the station-to-station gap would exceed 60 mi, too far for a loaded electric semi.

Penske overlay: 154 Penske facilities in Texas are classified by electrification risk tier based on their proximity to charging gaps.

Data sources: AFDC (US Dept. of Energy), OpenStreetMap, TIGER/Line, Penske Truck Rental.
